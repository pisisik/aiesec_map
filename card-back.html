💛 Our Support

Du bist nie allein unterwegs

Our AIESEC members in both your home and host countries will make sure to be there every step of the way — before leaving and while you are abroad.

✈️
Logistics Support
We ensure you get the right visa, arrival pick-up information before you travel and departure instructions when going back.
🌏
Learning Experience
We set clear expectations, provide cultural preparation and facilitate learning spaces before, during and after your experience.
🏠
Safe Living Conditions
Life and health insurance is mandatory. You will get information about accommodation and basic living costs before departure.
📋
Clear Job Description
We accompany you to the workplace on the first day and ensure your job description, goals and working hours are as expected.
